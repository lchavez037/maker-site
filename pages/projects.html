Maker Projects at the Danforth Center

All projects for focus on creating low-cost hardware and custom software for high-throughput plant phenotyping (imaging and characterization).

Four DDPSC-funded projects were started in 2014, PhenoStat, PhenoKinect, SoilSense, and PhenoDrone.

Please check the Maker Blog and Tutorials for updates.
